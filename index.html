About Aksharam

Aksharam Educational Charitable Trust was formed to provide world class quality education in Coimbatore comparable to education abroad. The trust is run by highly educated group that has right combination of Academicians and working Professionals in India & abroad. All the promoters have been very successful in their endeavors, have at least a master's degree, well-travelled the world and would like bring the best of education to Coimbatore with learnings from all over the globe. Promoters have rich experience in Teaching, research and Administration of Educational Institutions with National and International Exposure. The composition of the team itself is a testimony to the Quality of Education offered at Aksharam.

Aksharam Kids

Aksharam Kids is for juniors from 2 to 6 years old. Aksharam Kids is a play and study method with mix of Montessori and Preschool philosophies across the globe. Interactive teaching system helps kids to be at the center and learn through play model.

We have brought in best of teachers who are passionate about kids to help them learn and grow. Aksharam Kids is operational from Vijayadashami 2016. Aksharam Kids also has day care feature, call to know more. 

Aksharam Kids Specialties: 

- Activity and Play based learning inspired by Waldorf System

- 20 : 1 Student Teacher ratio

- No homeworks till 2nd grade.

- Learn at own pace and learn from group activities based method that makes children social and cognitive.

- All rounded education starting at young age including arts, crafts, yoga, brain power development, healthy living etc.

Aksharam International School

Aksharam International School is aims to provide world class education in Coimbatore. The school is located in scenic mountain area with focus on greenery. The school is aimed at capacity of 2500 students with best in class hostel facilities and latest amenities that will help a child grow and develop. The school will be targeted for both Indian and NRI kids. Our hostel will be equipped to house NRI kids with parents staying abroad. Call us or visit us to learn more.

We have paramount importance to extracurricular activities and we aim to bring in all-rounded children. Aksharam is a duly approved and CBSE accredited school with top notch syllabus.Aksharam International School will be operational from school year starting 2017


Aksharam International School Specialties:

- High end practical education up to 12th grade.

- Focus on social, economical, cultural and spiritual development.

- First Waldorf Inspired International School in Coimbatore.

- Top notch boarding facilities.

- Focus towards extracurricular and sports activities.

- Language Lab.

- Student led activity clubs.
