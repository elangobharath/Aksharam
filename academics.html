Core-Curriculam

’You don’t have to be great to get started, but you have to get started to be great .’’ 

Active teaching methodology purely based on “hands-on” and ‘’minds-on’’ activity. The teaching learning process stimulates the
learners to go beyond the boundaries of the text book. The
curriculum ensures the progress in the learning process of the child. 

Well – equipped and furnished class rooms with modern facilities.
Modern laboratories with latest scientific apparatus and innovative
instruments to gain the practical knowledge for better
understanding. 

Exposure to lot of academic related activities and co-curricular
activities. Good amenities & lot of extracurricular activities. 

Well – qualified & experienced teachers who are energetic, active,
optimistic and ever willing to walk the extra mile. 

Frequent field trips for real time experience and to broaden the
horizons of the students.   

Aksharam inculcate the principles of Waldorf Education along with the regular activities. 

To enable a child to develop in areas other than the academic, various club activities are organized to give an opportunity to the students to display their talents, such as Nature Club, Language Club, Astronomy Club, Photography Club, Animation Club, Art & Craft Club, Cyber Club, Maths Club, Science Club, Chess Club, Communication Club, etc. 

We have unbeatable recreation and sporting facilities with splendid soccer ground, basketball court, skating rink, badminton court and fascinating children’s play station. We ensure that all children have full and adequate access to the facilities that abound in the school. 

The smart class is an integral part of our curriculum and integrating
technology into curriculum is a priority at Aksharam. Technology oriented teaching has helped in offering a wide array of effective ways to reach different types of learners and multiple means to assess students.
