Say Hi.!

We welcome you to Aksharam International school, with a commitment to nurture and equip young learners with the uncommon ability of self exploration and development. Our aim is to nurture children to become confident, sensitive, socially committed and well rounded individuals, equipped to become young envoys of progress. Our children grow in the best of environment and under the guidance of competent faculty, elements indispensable today for gaining knowledge and understanding.

The process of admission reflects sensitivity and ease. Parents are given a guided tour of the school facilities. This is followed by an interaction of both the parents with the school Principal where a complete view of the philosophy, vision and methodology of the school is shared. Admission process is taken forward henceforth.

We would Love to have you on our Campus

Aksharam welcomes you to visit and take a guided tour with one of our administrators during school hours. Aksharam can accommodate tours daily and would love to have you on campus. Having a first hand look at a school while in session is a vital tool in evaluating education options and AIS is proud to have an open door policy. Prior Info is required. Tours are given Monday through Saturday by appointment. To schedule a tour, please fix appointment by emailing info@aksharamschool.org or calling (+91) 90955-99990 /91.
